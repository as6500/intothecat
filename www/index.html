How to play

In order to play the game, you first need an account to play. Its really easy to make one and we dont ask any personal information out of you.
The moment you have an account you can just log in, search for a game and thats it you're in!

Instructions

The game starts with each player on oposite sides of the board and you take turns moving around the board and attempting to hit each other with attacks.

You can only move and attack once with each pawn.

In order to move you click on your desired piece then the tile path you want it to take, then confirm. If the path is allowed it shall do it.

In order to attack you click on your desired piece then on the bottom left a attack button will appear, if you click on it then you may point your attack up, down, left or right.

if you ever feel like you dont want to either move or attack anymore this turn you may skip your turn at anytime on the top left.
